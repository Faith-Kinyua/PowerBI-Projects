🧩 <b> Problem Statement </b>

RetailCo’s sales team relied on Excel reports that took up to 2 days to update. By the time last week’s numbers were available, it was already too late to take corrective action. Store managers were making important decisions based on gut feeling rather than data, and leadership lacked real‑time visibility into performance across stores, products, and sales representatives.

To solve this, I built two connected Power BI dashboards that deliver instant insights, enabling faster, data‑driven decisions.

📊 <b> Executive Summary </b>

This dashboard provides leadership with a high‑level view of company performance, including revenue, profit, quantity sold, product performance, store contribution, and the impact of discounts on revenue.

💼 <b> Business Impact </b>

This solution transformed RetailCo’s reporting process from slow, reactive Excel reporting to real‑time, insight‑driven decision‑making. By replacing manual updates with automated Power BI dashboards, leadership and store managers can now:

<b> Monitor performance instantly </b>  
No more waiting two days for Excel updates — KPIs refresh automatically, enabling same‑day action.

<b> Identify top‑performing and underperforming stores </b>
Regional leaders can quickly see which locations are driving revenue and which require operational support.

<b> Evaluate product profitability and discount impact </b>
The Revenue vs Discounts visual helps the business understand how pricing decisions affect revenue and margins.

<b> Track sales representative productivity </b>
High performers like Adebayo Ogunlesi and Chioma Nwankwo are easily identified, enabling better coaching and recognition.

<b> Spot issues early and take corrective action </b> 
Target achievement metrics highlight stores falling behind, allowing managers to intervene before month‑end.

<b> Align executives and store managers around a single source of truth </b> 
Everyone now works from the same real‑time data, improving communication and strategic alignment.
